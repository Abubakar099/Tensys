Questions? We have answers

How is Tenex Engineering different from a dev shop?

Dev shops have a certain reputation. Mediocre, underpaid offshore talent producing sub-par software. That is not Tenex Engineering. Our team is composed of NYC-based 10x engineers, who are incentivized to ship elegant software at exceptional speed for clients ranging from VC-backed startups to multi-generational enterprises.

What does AI transformation mean?

The process by which we identify your company’s biggest bottlenecks, create scalable & effective AI-driven solutions to increase profits, train & upskill your people to use these solutions reliably, and repeat the virtuous cycle.

How does pricing work?

Depends. For Tenex Engineering, we charge a monthly subscription based on output. For Tenex Transformation, we build a custom partnership that ranges in time & price, based on scope. Let’s hop on a call to get you a concrete answer.

What’s different about Tenex compared to other consultancies?

We only care about one thing: business impact. No 200-slide strategy decks. No 6-month diagnostics. No strategy without implementation. We’re just a group of proven builders helping you to win the next decade.

How do your AI engineering services work?

We offer outcome‑based, subscription‑style product and engineering squads that leverage AI acceleration to ship production‑grade software faster and more affordably— you pay for features delivered, not hours logged.

What kind of companies do you typically work with?

At-scale companies (>$25 million revenue) that are motivated to become AI-first, but lack the resources internally to make that happen.
